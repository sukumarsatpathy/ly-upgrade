Frequently Asked Questions
Why should I pay conference registration fees in advance?

Due to the high demand worldwide for the Laughter Yoga World Conference, bookings are filling up fast. Bookings will be closed once we reach maximum auditorium capacity, and the only way to guarantee your seat is to register now.

Will the conference be live-streamed as well?

No. It is an in-person conference in Nashik, India.

What is the conference duration?

It is a five-day event that begins on Monday, 16th October 2023 and concludes on Friday, 20th October 2023.

Does the conference registration fee include travel, food and accommodation?

No. $375 is ONLY for registering and booking your conference seat.

When should I start preparing for the conference?

You can start planning at your convenience. As soon as you register and book your conference seat, you can also check/book flight tickets in advance from your country to Nashik, India.

When should I arrive at the venue?

You can arrive one day before, i.e. on 15th October 2023, to get accustomed to the climate and be fresh before the big event. And if you are planning to tour India before the conference, then ensure you arrive at the conference on time.

What are the preferred travel routes to Nashik, India?

  • Route Option A: Land at New Delhi Airport and take another flight to the city of Nashik.
  • Route Option B: Land at Mumbai Airport and book a taxi for a 5-hour drive to the city of Nashik.

How to get more information about the Visa process?

You can access this page for application for a tourist e-visa: https://indianvisaonline.gov.in/evisa/tvoa.html

When and how can I pay and book food and accommodation?

The accommodation booking process will start later, and you will be informed in advance through email on the email id you used to register for the conference.

What if I don't get accommodation?

Your accommodation will be 100% confirmed when you pay the $375 conference fees and register your seat for the conference.

What accommodation types are available, and what are their charges?

We have a discounted package deal with the hotel as follows:

  • Deluxe Rooms: $85 per person/night
  • Luxury Villa: $110 per person/night

Please note:

  • These are all-meals packages
  • Packages are two-person sharing
  • Single-person accommodations are unavailable

The accommodation booking process will start later, and you will be informed in advance through email on the email id you used to register for the conference.

What if I want a single room for myself?

You can book your room outside the conference venue independently. You can contact us, and we will provide you with hotel details.
