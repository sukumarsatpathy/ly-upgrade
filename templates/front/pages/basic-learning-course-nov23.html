What will you learn in the Basic Learning Course

Laugh, Love, Live: Harnessing the Benefits of Laughter Yoga for a Fulfilling Life

It is the goal of this coaching to increase the amount of laughter and joy in your life by practicing laughter yoga on a daily basis and thereby gain physical, mental, and emotional benefits. It will enable you to perform laughter exercises at home and share them with your family and friends after you have learned the techniques.

Many scientific studies have proven that laughter and joy can help reduce stress, boost the immune system, and even help with depression. Practicing laughter yoga helps to release endorphins, which are feel-good hormones, into the body. This can help to improve overall wellbeing.

Frequently Asked Questions
Why do I need Laughter Yoga Course?

Do you feel stressed out regularly, out of breath? Or are you having chronic pain or illness? For centuries we have known that laughter is the best medicine. And laughter yoga is the only platform where you can get all the scientifically proven health benefits of laughter.

Laughter yoga exercises relieve pain, boost immunity, improve mood besides many other social, emotional and physiological benefits.

Laughter yoga is the gateway to your health, happiness and peace of mind.

Does Dr. Madan Kataria himself conducts these trainings?

Yes. Dr. Madan Kataria founded Laughter Yoga and he conducts these training programs worldwide.

Who is eligible to receive a Leader Training Certification?

Once you have completed the six sessions of the Basic Learning Course, you are eligible for the Leader Course. This is an additional six sessions.

The Basic Learning Course teaches you how to bring more joy and laughter into your life and loved ones. And when you complete the Leader Course, you can start your own Online or Physical Free Laughter Clubs as well as become eligible to train others.

Is there any prerequisite knowledge required before enrolling in this Basic Learning Course?

No prerequisites are needed! You just need to laugh and we will teach you all the skills.

Am I able to bring my family or friends to the live sessions?

Ofcourse yes. Anyone can join these online training programs. However, only the registered member is eligible for the certification.

I would like to join the WhatsApp group. What should I do?

As soon as you pay and register for the program, you will receive an automatic email from Laughter Yoga International. This email contains the link to join the WhatsApp group.

If you still face any issues, please write to us at help@laughteryoga.org or connect with us on (+91) 888 456 7891.

Even if I cannot attend the Live sessions, will I receive the video recordings?

Yes. All sessions are recorded and shared. You will also receive a YouTube playlist of all the sessions organised for your future reference.

Do I have to complete homework assignments during the course?

You will not find any other training program where the only homework is for you to laugh more! We have a 40-day laughter challenge which is fun to do and builds your confidence to laugh more!

Following the completion of this course, what further learning opportunities are there in laughter yoga?

Laughter yoga is in high demand worldwide. It is spread in more than 110 countries. After you become a certified leader, you can participate in the five-day in-person Teacher Training program conducted by the founder Dr. Madan Kataria, himself in Nashik.

Dr. Madan Kataria also conducts online mentorship program for Laughter Yoga Professionals worldwide - Laughter Yoga 2.0.

In what way can my knowledge and skills from this course be applied in my daily life?

Laughter yoga leaders and facilitators are in demand for conducting online and in-person sessions for corporates, senior citizen centers, schools, hospitals, and at many other applications. You will learn all the techniques during the courses.
