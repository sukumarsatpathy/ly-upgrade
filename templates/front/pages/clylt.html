Frequently Asked Questions
Can I enroll only in the Certified Leader Training Course?

Leader Training Combo Course is of total 12 lessons i.e. 6 lessons of Basic Learning Course and then 6 additional lessons of Certified Leader Training Course. It is mandatory to learn both to get a leader certification.

Who is eligible for Leader Training Certification?

Once you have completed the six sessions of the Basic Learning Course, you are eligible for the Leader Course. This is an additional six sessions.

Can I register only for the Basic Learning Course?

Yes. The Basic Learning Course teaches you how to bring more joy and laughter into your life and loved ones. And when you complete the Leader Course, you can start your own Online or Physical Free Laughter Clubs as well as become eligible to train others.

Does Dr. Madan Kataria himself conducts these trainings?

Yes. Dr. Madan Kataria founded Laughter Yoga and he conducts laughter yoga training programs worldwide.

I would like to join the WhatsApp group. What should I do?

As soon as you pay and register for the program, you will receive an automatic email from Laughter Yoga International. This email contains the link to join the WhatsApp group.

If you still face any issues, please write to us at help@laughteryoga.org or connect with us on (+91) 888 456 7891.

Even if I cannot attend the Live sessions, will I receive the video recordings?

Yes. All sessions are recorded and shared. You will also receive a YouTube playlist of all the sessions organized for your future reference.

Following the completion of this course, what further learning opportunities are there in laughter yoga?

Laughter yoga is in high demand worldwide. It is spread in more than 120 countries. After you become a certified leader, you can participate in the five-day in-person Teacher Training program conducted by the founder Dr. Madan Kataria, himself in Nashik.

Dr. Madan Kataria also conducts an online mentorship program for Laughter Yoga Professionals worldwide - Laughter Yoga 2.0.

In what way can my knowledge and skills from this course be applied in my daily life?

Laughter yoga leaders and facilitators are in demand for conducting online and in-person sessions for corporates, senior citizen centers, schools, hospitals, and at many other applications. You will learn all the techniques during the courses.
